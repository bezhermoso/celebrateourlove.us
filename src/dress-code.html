THINGS YU COULD WEAR

We require everyone attending to wear formal attire. Think dress to impress!

We always find dress codes a bit confusing, so here is a guide to what you could wear.

SHE SHOULD WEAR

A long, flowy dress, or a formal, cocktail-length dress in a light tone.

HE SHOULD WEAR

A formal suit & tie. Grey or navy blue is preferred.
