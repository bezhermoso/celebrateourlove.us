Travel & Accomodation

If travelling to the Philippines, please avoid connecting flights to Ninoy Aquino International Airport (NAIA). Read more

We recommend the following airlines: Asiana Airlines, Korean Airlines, and Cathay Pacific. These airlines has flights with Cebu as point-of-entry into the Philippines, allowing you to avoid NAIA and any subsequent connecting domestic flights.
